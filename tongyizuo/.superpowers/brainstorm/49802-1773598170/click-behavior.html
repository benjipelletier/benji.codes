You're zoomed into a cluster. You click a word. What happens?

This one decision shapes the whole experience.

🚀

A — Navigate to the cluster page

Click = leave the galaxy, go to the existing rich cluster page (collocations, challenge mode, full detail). The galaxy explorer is the discovery layer, the cluster page is the study layer.

Simple. Two clear modes. Click = commit to studying that cluster.

B — Slide-up detail panel (stay on map)

Click = a panel slides up from the bottom (like Google Maps tapping a location). Shows the word's core scene, glosses, and a "Study this cluster →" button. The galaxy stays visible behind it. Dismissing the panel returns you to exploring.

Stays immersive. Preview before committing to study.

Click = the cluster expands right there on the canvas. Edge labels animate in, showing the connecting glosses. A floating card shows the selected word's core scene. You study inside the explorer. "Navigate to cluster" is a secondary button if you want collocations/challenge.

Most immersive. The map and the lesson are the same thing.
