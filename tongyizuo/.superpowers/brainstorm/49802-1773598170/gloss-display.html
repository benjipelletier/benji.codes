How should the connecting glosses show?

The gloss is the reason two words are related. How visible should it be?

A — Always-on edge labels
Every edge shows its gloss as a small pill floating on the line. Always readable, no interaction needed.
No interaction needed · can get cluttered with many edges
B — Hover to reveal
Edges glow on hover and show the gloss tooltip. Clean at a glance, informative on demand.
Clean · requires mouse interaction · touch-unfriendly
C — Select a word → its glosses radiate
Clicking a word highlights all its edges and shows every connecting gloss simultaneously. Other edges dim. Tapping empty space resets.
Most informative · works on touch · game-like selection feel
